## Purpose

Define shared business and commercial terminology used across Gojen Product Office documentation.

## Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **ARR** | Annual Recurring Revenue — contracted recurring revenue normalized to a one-year period. |
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue — recurring revenue normalized to a one-month period. (Distinct from the `MRR` document prefix for Market Research Report.) |
| **Churn** | Rate at which customers or revenue leave in a period. |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost — fully loaded cost to acquire one customer. |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value — expected net revenue from a customer over the relationship. |
| **ICP** | Ideal Customer Profile — description of the best-fit customer segment. |
| **CVP** | Customer Value Proposition — statement of value delivered to a target customer. |
| **BMC** | Business Model Canvas / commercial model framing for how value is created and captured. |
| **GTM** | Go-to-Market — plan for launching and selling a product or capability. |
| **TAM / SAM / SOM** | Total / Serviceable / Obtainable Market — nested market sizing views. |
| **KPI** | Key Performance Indicator — metric used to evaluate success against objectives. |
| **OKR** | Objectives and Key Results — goal-setting framework linking outcomes to measurable results. |
| **Founder Board** | Highest accountability body for Gojen Technology direction reflected in this repository. |
| **Product Office** | Function that maintains documentation standards, templates, and portfolio documentation integrity. |
| **Monthly Business Review** | Recurring management review of commercial and portfolio health. |
| **Quarterly Strategy Review** | Recurring strategy reassessment of vision and portfolio priorities. |
