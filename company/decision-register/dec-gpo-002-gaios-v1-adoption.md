## Options Considered

| Option | Summary | Pros | Cons |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A — Adopt GAIOS v1.0 | Full OS framework in-repo | Coherent, AI-ready, scalable | Upfront documentation investment |
| B — Informal founder notes only | Minimal process | Fast short-term | Does not scale; high fragmentation risk |
| C — Wait for more headcount | Defer OS design | Less writing now | Rework later under pressure |
| D — External OS product | Buy third-party ops tool | Packaged UX | Not Git-native; SSOT conflict |

## Decision

Adopt **Option A — GAIOS v1.0**. New GAIOS folders and documents are additive; existing company standards and product workspaces remain untouched unless explicitly migrated in a later approved change.

## Reason

- Aligns with DEC-GPO-001 (GitHub SSOT).
- Creates consistent metadata, navigation, and approval patterns.
- Enables AI agents to operate with explicit rules and memory locations.
