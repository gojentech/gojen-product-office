## Working Competitive Map

| Category | Observation | Implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Incumbents | Broader suites may be heavy for focused operators | Compete on clarity and workflow fit |
| Point tools | Narrow tools create integration debt | Compete on coherent operating flow |
| Status quo | Manual processes remain sticky | Onboarding and trust matter as much as features |
| Emerging | AI-assisted ops may reshape expectations | Document AI usage and data control early |

## Differentiation Hypotheses

1. Documentation-led product quality and decision transparency.
2. Founder-proximate iteration speed with disciplined gates.
3. Clear MVP boundary — solve the critical path before expanding.
