## When to Use

Use when mapping competitors for Subscription OS or Pawn Management before positioning or roadmap decisions.

## Prompt Body

```text
Build a competitive landscape brief for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Segment: [SEGMENT]
Competitors: [LIST or "discover"]
Our differentiators hypothesis: [HYPOTHESIS]

Deliver:
1. Competitor cards (positioning, ICP, pricing model if known, strengths, weaknesses)
2. Feature comparison matrix (Must / Differentiator / Parity)
3. White-space opportunities
4. Threat ranking with early signals
5. Implications for Gojen roadmap and sales messaging

Cite assumptions explicitly.
```

## Expected Output

Competitor cards, feature matrix, white-space opportunities, threat ranking, and roadmap/sales implications.

## Restrictions

- Do not invent pricing; mark as unknown.
- Do not disparage competitors with unverified claims.
- Do not treat rumor as fact; separate confirmed vs hypothesized.
