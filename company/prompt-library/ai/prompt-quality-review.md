## When to Use

Use when reviewing a new or updated prompt before adding it to the Gojen Prompt Library.

## Prompt Body

```text
Review this prompt for inclusion in Gojen Technology Prompt Library.

Prompt draft: [DRAFT]
Intended users: [USERS]
Related product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management | Company]

Score and improve:
1. Clarity of when-to-use
2. Completeness of inputs
3. Output specificity
4. Restrictions adequacy
5. Cross-link readiness
6. Reuse potential

Return: scorecard, revised prompt body, and go/no-go recommendation.
```

## Expected Output

Scorecard, revised prompt body, and go/no-go recommendation for library inclusion.

## Restrictions

- Do not approve prompts that encourage fabricating facts.
- Do not approve prompts without restrictions.
- Do not remove governance constraints to make prompts "easier".
