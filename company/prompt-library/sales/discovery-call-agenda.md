## When to Use

Use before discovery calls with prospects evaluating Subscription OS or Pawn Management.

## Prompt Body

```text
Create a discovery call agenda for Gojen Technology sales.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Prospect: [COMPANY / ROLE]
Known context: [CONTEXT]
Call length: [MINUTES]

Deliver:
1. Call objective (one sentence)
2. Timed agenda
3. Discovery questions mapped to MEDDICC / BANT elements as appropriate
4. Demo hooks (only if earned)
5. Mutual next-step options
6. Disqualify signals

Focus on learning, not pitching.
```

## Expected Output

Timed discovery agenda with questions, demo hooks, next steps, and disqualify signals.

## Restrictions

- Do not promise custom features without product confirmation.
- Do not share confidential pricing outside approved bands.
- Do not skip qualification in favor of feature dump.
