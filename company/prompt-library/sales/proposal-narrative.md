## When to Use

Use when drafting a commercial proposal narrative for Subscription OS or Pawn Management.

## Prompt Body

```text
Draft a proposal narrative for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Prospect pains: [PAINS]
Proposed scope: [SCOPE]
Success metrics: [METRICS]
Commercial model: [MODEL]
Timeline: [TIMELINE]

Structure:
1. Executive summary
2. Understanding of the problem
3. Proposed solution and scope boundaries
4. Implementation approach
5. Value and ROI logic
6. Commercial terms summary (high level)
7. Risks and mutual responsibilities
8. Clear call to action

Professional, specific, and buyer-centric.
```

## Expected Output

Buyer-centric proposal narrative with scope, value logic, commercial summary, and CTA.

## Restrictions

- Do not invent discounts or SLAs.
- Do not include legal terms beyond approved boilerplate summaries.
- Do not overscope beyond what delivery can commit.
