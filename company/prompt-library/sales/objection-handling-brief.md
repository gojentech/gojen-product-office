## When to Use

Use when preparing responses to common sales objections for Gojen products.

## Prompt Body

```text
Create an objection-handling brief for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Objections: [LIST]
Competitor mentioned: [NAME or none]
Our proof points: [PROOF]

For each objection:
1. Likely underlying concern
2. Clarifying question
3. Response narrative
4. Proof / case / demo to use
5. When to walk away

Keep responses honest and non-manipulative.
```

## Expected Output

Per-objection briefs with clarifying questions, responses, proof points, and walk-away criteria.

## Restrictions

- Do not make false competitive claims.
- Do not pressure tactics.
- Do not contradict published product capabilities.
