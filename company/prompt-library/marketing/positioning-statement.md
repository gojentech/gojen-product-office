## When to Use

Use when crafting or refreshing positioning for Subscription OS or Pawn Management.

## Prompt Body

```text
Craft positioning for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
ICP: [ICP]
Category: [CATEGORY]
Primary alternative: [ALTERNATIVE]
Key value: [VALUE]
Proof: [PROOF]

Deliver:
1. One-sentence positioning statement
2. Elevator pitch (30 seconds)
3. Message pillars (3)
4. Proof points per pillar
5. Anti-positioning (what we are not)
6. Internal alignment notes for sales and product

Make it distinctive and usable.
```

## Expected Output

Positioning statement, elevator pitch, three message pillars with proof, and anti-positioning.

## Restrictions

- Do not claim unproven market leadership.
- Do not use buzzword stacks without meaning.
- Do not conflict with approved brand voice.
