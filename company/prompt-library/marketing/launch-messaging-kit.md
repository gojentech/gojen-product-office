## When to Use

Use when preparing messaging for a feature or product launch for Gojen Technology.

## Prompt Body

```text
Build a launch messaging kit for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Launch item: [FEATURE]
Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Launch date target: [DATE]
Constraints: [LEGAL / EMBARGO]

Produce:
1. Launch thesis
2. External announcement draft
3. Email / LinkedIn / in-app copy variants
4. FAQ for sales and support
5. Screenshot / demo talking points
6. Success metrics for the launch

Keep claims accurate to shipped scope.
```

## Expected Output

Launch thesis, multi-channel copy, FAQ, demo talking points, and launch success metrics.

## Restrictions

- Do not announce unshipped capabilities as available.
- Do not ignore embargo or legal review flags.
- Do not reuse competitor trademarks improperly.
