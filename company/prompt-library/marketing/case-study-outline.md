## When to Use

Use when turning a customer success story into a publishable case study outline.

## Prompt Body

```text
Outline a case study for Gojen Technology.

Product: [Subscription OS | Pawn Management]
Customer (or anonymized): [CUSTOMER]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Problem: [PROBLEM]
Outcomes: [OUTCOMES]
Permission status: [APPROVED / PENDING]

Structure:
1. Headline and subhead
2. Customer snapshot
3. Challenge
4. Solution journey
5. Results (quantified where allowed)
6. Quote placement
7. CTA

Flag any claim needing customer approval.
```

## Expected Output

Publishable case study outline with quantified results flags and approval callouts.

## Restrictions

- Do not publish without permission confirmation.
- Do not invent ROI numbers.
- Do not include confidential operational details.
