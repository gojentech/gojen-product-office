## When to Use

Use before board or advisor updates to craft a coherent narrative of progress, risks, and asks for Gojen Technology.

## Prompt Body

```text
Draft a board narrative for Gojen Technology.

Period: [PERIOD]
Highlights: [HIGHLIGHTS]
Metrics: [ARR / PIPELINE / NPS / UPTIME / RELEASES]
Product progress: Subscription OS [SOS]; Pawn Management [PAW]
Risks and incidents: [RISKS]
Asks of the board: [ASKS]

Structure:
1. Opening thesis (2â€“3 sentences)
2. Traction and truth (metrics with interpretation)
3. Product progress by line
4. Risks and mitigations
5. Capital / hiring / partnership asks
6. Decisions requested from the board

Write in confident, plain language suitable for directors.
```

## Expected Output

A board-ready narrative covering thesis, metrics interpretation, product progress, risks, asks, and requested decisions.

## Restrictions

- Do not fabricate metrics; use placeholders where data is missing.
- Do not blame individuals; focus on systems and decisions.
- Do not include confidential legal strategy details beyond what was provided.
