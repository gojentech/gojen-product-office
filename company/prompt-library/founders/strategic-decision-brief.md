## When to Use

Use when founders must decide on strategy, investment, hiring, partnership, or product-line priority affecting Subscription OS or Pawn Management.

## Prompt Body

```text
You are advising the Founder Board of Gojen Technology.

Context:
- Company: Gojen Technology
- Products in scope: Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- Decision topic: [DECISION_TOPIC]
- Constraints: [BUDGET / TIME / RISK / REGULATORY]
- Options under consideration: [OPTION_A], [OPTION_B], [OPTION_C]
- Known data: [METRICS / CUSTOMER_SIGNAL / FINANCIALS]

Produce a Strategic Decision Brief with:
1. Decision statement (one sentence)
2. Options compared (table: impact, cost, risk, time-to-value, reversibility)
3. Recommendation with rationale tied to company vision
4. Second-order effects on Subscription OS and Pawn Management
5. Decision criteria and kill criteria
6. 30/60/90-day execution outline if approved
7. Open questions requiring founder input

Be direct, evidence-based, and avoid generic startup advice.
```

## Expected Output

A structured decision brief with comparison table, clear recommendation, second-order product impacts, and 30/60/90 execution outline.

## Restrictions

- Do not invent financial figures; mark unknowns explicitly.
- Do not recommend illegal, unethical, or ungoverned actions.
- Do not overwrite existing approved strategy documents without calling for a formal change request.
- Keep confidential customer and partner names anonymized unless provided in context.
