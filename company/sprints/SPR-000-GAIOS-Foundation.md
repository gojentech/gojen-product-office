## Sprint goal

Establish GAIOS v1.0 as the navigable, governed company operating system for Gojen Technology so founders, Product Office stewards, and AI assistants share one SSOT in GitHub.

---

## Objectives achieved

1. Created GAIOS entry points: START-HERE, INDEX, SEARCH.
2. Delivered AI governance pack (rules, workflows, lifecycles, matrices, standards).
3. Delivered memory pack and operating surfaces (workspaces, dashboards, registers, playbooks, agents, wiki, learning, quality, versions, product portfolio layer).
4. Preserved existing Product Office standards without modification.
5. Secured Founder Board approval of GAIOS as the official operating system.

## Lessons learned

- Separate active sprint status from permanent history; archive closed sprints under `company/sprints/`.
- Additive-only creation protects existing standards and avoids accidental overwrite.
- Document IDs and START-HERE back-links keep a large corpus navigable for humans and AI.
- Portfolio summaries under `company/products/` must link to authoritative root `products/` workspaces.
