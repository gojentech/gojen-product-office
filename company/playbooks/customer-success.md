## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **Customer Success** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Review health scores and red accounts for both products
- [ ] Respond to critical support escalations within SLA
- [ ] Log product feedback with severity and customer context
- [ ] Confirm onboarding tasks for new customers are on schedule
- [ ] Update CRM/CS notes after every meaningful customer touch

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Portfolio health review (adoption, usage, sentiment)
- [ ] QBR preparation for accounts due this month
- [ ] Escalation trend report to Product and Engineering
- [ ] Renewal and expansion forecast input to Sales
- [ ] Playbook gaps identified from recent tickets

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Retention and churn root-cause analysis
- [ ] Onboarding time-to-value trend review
- [ ] CSAT/NPS action planning
- [ ] Case study candidates with Marketing ([GOS-GPO-191](../prompt-library/marketing/case-study-outline.md))
- [ ] Success plan template improvements

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Segment strategy refresh (Subscription OS vs Pawn Management customers)
- [ ] Capacity model for CS coverage
- [ ] Voice-of-customer package for Product quarterly planning
- [ ] SLA and support tier review with Operations and Finance
- [ ] Publish CS outcomes and top systemic product issues
