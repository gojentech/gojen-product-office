## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **Sales** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Update CRM stages for every active Subscription OS and Pawn Management opportunity
- [ ] Complete discovery or follow-up commitments from prior day
- [ ] Log objections and competitive mentions for marketing/product feedback
- [ ] Qualify or disqualify stalled leads using MEDDICC / BANT as appropriate
- [ ] Prepare agendas for today''s calls using [GOS-GPO-186](../prompt-library/sales/discovery-call-agenda.md)

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Pipeline review: coverage, slip risk, next steps by opportunity
- [ ] Forecast commit vs. upside for the month/quarter
- [ ] Win/loss notes synthesis for Product and Marketing
- [ ] Proposal quality check using [GOS-GPO-187](../prompt-library/sales/proposal-narrative.md)
- [ ] Align with Customer Success on handoff readiness for late-stage deals

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Quota attainment and ramp analysis
- [ ] ICP fit audit of closed-won and closed-lost
- [ ] Pricing exception review with Finance and CEO
- [ ] Enablement update (decks, demos, objection briefs via [GOS-GPO-188](../prompt-library/sales/objection-handling-brief.md))
- [ ] Territory / segment focus adjustments for Subscription OS vs Pawn Management

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Quarterly sales plan vs. actuals
- [ ] Competitive displacement review with Research prompts
- [ ] Compensation and capacity planning input to Finance
- [ ] GTM messaging alignment with Marketing Director
- [ ] Strategic account plan refresh for top customers and prospects
