## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **Product** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Clear product decisions blocking engineering
- [ ] Review incoming customer insights routed from Sales and CS
- [ ] Validate today''s release notes / feature flag plan if shipping
- [ ] Keep PRD and story clarity above threshold for in-sprint work
- [ ] Track outcomes, not just output, for live experiments

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Backlog refinement for Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- [ ] Roadmap risk review (dependencies, research gaps)
- [ ] Analytics instrumentation check for recently shipped work
- [ ] Stakeholder sync (Sales, CS, Engineering) on commitments
- [ ] Story quality pass using [GOS-GPO-175](../prompt-library/product/user-story-refinement.md)

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Outcome review vs. OKRs and success metrics
- [ ] Discovery pipeline health (research, interviews, experiments)
- [ ] Roadmap tradeoff session using [GOS-GPO-176](../prompt-library/product/roadmap-tradeoff-analysis.md)
- [ ] Competitive and market signal intake from Research
- [ ] Documentation completeness audit with Documentation Engineer

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Product strategy review per product line
- [ ] Portfolio balance across growth, retention, and platform
- [ ] Pricing/packaging input with Sales and Finance
- [ ] Major PRD portfolio health check via [GOS-GPO-174](../prompt-library/product/prd-outline-generator.md)
- [ ] Publish next-quarter themes and non-goals
