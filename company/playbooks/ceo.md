## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **CEO** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Review overnight alerts for Subscription OS and Pawn Management production health
- [ ] Scan priority inbox and escalate blockers to owners within two hours
- [ ] Confirm top three company outcomes for the day remain on track
- [ ] Clear decisions waiting on CEO authority (hiring, spend, customer exceptions)
- [ ] Sync briefly with CPO / Engineering Manager / Sales Director as needed
- [ ] Capture decisions in the decision register with owners and due dates

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Lead or review weekly leadership standup across product lines
- [ ] Review pipeline vs. forecast with Sales Director
- [ ] Review delivery health (sprint burndown, incidents, quality) with Engineering and Product
- [ ] Update founder weekly priorities using [GOS-GPO-172](../prompt-library/founders/founder-weekly-priorities.md)
- [ ] Approve or reject material scope changes for Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- [ ] Check cash, runway, and hiring plan with Finance Lead

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Company KPI review (growth, retention, delivery, quality, cash)
- [ ] Customer health deep-dive with Customer Success
- [ ] Organizational capacity and hiring funnel review
- [ ] Risk register review and mitigation ownership refresh
- [ ] Board/advisor narrative draft check using [GOS-GPO-173](../prompt-library/founders/board-narrative-draft.md)

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Strategy refresh: market, competitive, and product-line priorities
- [ ] Quarterly OKR scoring and next-quarter OKR draft
- [ ] Portfolio investment decision between Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- [ ] Leadership performance and succession notes
- [ ] Major partnership / pricing / positioning decisions
- [ ] Publish quarterly outcomes to company wiki and update related playbooks if cadence changed
