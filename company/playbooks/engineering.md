## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **Engineering** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Review CI status and failing checks for Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- [ ] Triage Sev incidents and customer-impacting bugs
- [ ] Unblock engineers (reviews, decisions, environments)
- [ ] Confirm today''s commit plan vs. sprint board
- [ ] Scan security alerts and assign owners

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Sprint progress and risk review with Tech Lead and Product Owner
- [ ] Code review latency and PR aging report
- [ ] Quality metrics (escape defects, flaky tests) with QA Lead
- [ ] On-call handoff and incident trend check with DevOps
- [ ] Architecture debt items: promote or defer with Solution Architect

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Engineering KPI review (throughput, lead time, change fail rate, MTTR)
- [ ] Hiring and leveling calibration
- [ ] Platform / reliability investment proposal
- [ ] Dependency and license risk review
- [ ] Postmortem action item aging audit using [GOS-GPO-179](../prompt-library/engineering/incident-postmortem-draft.md)

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Engineering strategy alignment with Product roadmap
- [ ] Skill matrix and succession for critical modules
- [ ] Major tech bets for Subscription OS and Pawn Management
- [ ] Capacity allocation across product lines
- [ ] Standards and Definition of Done refresh
