## Purpose

This playbook defines the operating rhythm for **Operations** at Gojen Technology across Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Check operational blockers across hiring, vendors, facilities, and tooling
- [ ] Confirm meeting rooms / remote rituals running as scheduled
- [ ] Track open procurement and access requests
- [ ] Verify critical SaaS admin health (identity, email, storage)
- [ ] Escalate cross-team process failures same day

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Process SLA review (ticket aging, onboarding cycle time)
- [ ] Vendor invoice and contract checkpoint with Finance
- [ ] Meeting effectiveness spot-check (agendas and minutes present)
- [ ] Update operating calendar for the next two weeks
- [ ] Coordinate with DevOps on non-prod access hygiene

## Monthly Checklist

- [ ] Policy and SOP refresh for company operations
- [ ] Capacity planning for shared services supporting both products
- [ ] Audit of access reviews and least-privilege compliance
- [ ] Cost optimization opportunities with Finance Lead
- [ ] Quarterly vendor performance notes

## Quarterly Review

- [ ] Operating model retrospective
- [ ] Headcount operations plan input for next quarter
- [ ] Risk and compliance checkpoint with CEO and Finance
- [ ] Tooling portfolio rationalization
- [ ] Publish operations scorecard to leadership
