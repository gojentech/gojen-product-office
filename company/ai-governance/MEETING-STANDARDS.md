## Relationship to GPO-STD-004

[GPO-STD-004 Meeting Process](../standards/meeting-process.md) remains the authoritative Product Office meeting process. This GAIOS document adds AI-aware requirements: pre-read packs, decision capture into GitHub, and explicit treatment of AI-generated notes as drafts until a human owner accepts them.

---

## Meeting types

| Type | Purpose | Typical owners |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Founder Board | Strategy, priority, material approvals | Founders |
| Sprint ceremonies | Plan, review, retro for GAIOS or product sprints | Product Office / product leads |
| Working session | Deep work with optional AI collaborator | Topic owner |
| Decision clinic | Resolve a single decision with RACI | Accountable role |

---

## Required artifacts

| Artifact | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| Agenda | Published before the meeting with goals and pre-reads |
| Pre-reads | Link to START-HERE, CURRENT-SPRINT, or product docs as relevant |
| Decisions | Capture via [DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md](./DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md); never leave material decisions only in chat |
| Actions | Owner, due date, and success condition |
| Minutes | Human-accepted record; AI drafts are not final |

Existing company minutes live under [company/meeting-minutes/](../meeting-minutes/README.md). Product minutes live under each product’s `meeting-minutes/`. Planned GAIOS `company/meetings/` may index and link these without replacing them.

---

## AI in meetings

- AI may draft agendas, summarize transcripts, and propose action lists.
- AI must label drafts as drafts until a human owner accepts.
- AI must not invent attendance, votes, or approvals.
- Durable outcomes must be written to the repository after the meeting.

---

## Minimum agenda template

1. Goal of the meeting
2. Pre-reads (links)
3. Decisions required (if any)
4. Discussion
5. Actions and owners
6. Confirm what will be committed to GitHub
