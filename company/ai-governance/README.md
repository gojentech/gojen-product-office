## Purpose

`ai-governance/` is the control plane for how AI assistants and humans collaborate inside Gojen Technology’s Product Office. It defines context loading, session discipline, prompts, meetings, sprints, document and decision lifecycles, roles, RACI, and GAIOS-specific standards that extend — but do not replace — [GPO-STD-001](../standards/document-numbering.md) through [GPO-STD-005](../standards/repository-rules.md).
