## Quality rules

1. Markdown only for GAIOS doctrine files.
2. No lorem ipsum, no empty placeholder sections, no “TBD” as a stand-in for content.
3. Prefer precise relative links.
4. Use Mermaid when it clarifies workflows, lifecycles, or structure.
5. Cross-reference related GOS-GPO and GPO-STD documents.
6. Keep Purpose and Scope honest and narrow.
7. When adding future documents, update [INDEX.md](../INDEX.md) and [SEARCH.md](../SEARCH.md).
