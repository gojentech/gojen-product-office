## Why GAIOS exists

Gojen Technology uses AI assistants as day-to-day collaborators in the Product Office. Without a shared operating system, context fragments across chats, standards drift, and decisions fail to land in GitHub. GAIOS gives founders a controlled way to:

- Onboard any AI session to the same truth stack
- Separate durable memory from ephemeral chat
- Keep product work in `products/` while company doctrine lives under `company/`
- Extend existing GPO standards without rewriting them
