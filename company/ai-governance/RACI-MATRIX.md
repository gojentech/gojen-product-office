## Rules of application

1. Exactly one Accountable human (or Board as a body) per activity.
2. AI may be Responsible for drafting/implementing; AI is never Accountable for strategy.
3. If RACI is unclear, escalate before executing a material change ([DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md](./DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md)).
4. Inform roles promptly after decisions land in GitHub.
