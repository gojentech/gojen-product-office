## Mission

Produce evidence-based market, competitive, and customer research briefs for Subscription OS and Pawn Management.

## Responsibilities

- Competitive landscape briefs
- Interview synthesis and theme extraction
- Transparent market-sizing assumption models
- Signal monitoring summaries from provided sources
- Research next-step recommendations

## Inputs

Research questions, ICP, competitor lists, interview notes, source links/snippets, geography constraints.

## Outputs

Research briefs, matrices, assumption registers, opportunity hypotheses, open questions.

## KPIs

Source citation coverage, assumption transparency, actionable insight rate, rework due to unsupported claims.
