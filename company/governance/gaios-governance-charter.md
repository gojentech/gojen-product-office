## Charter Statement

GAIOS is Gojen Technology’s internal operating system for how founders, employees, and AI assistants create, decide, document, and execute. It is not a customer-facing software product. GitHub is the system of record ([DEC-GPO-001](../decision-register/dec-gpo-001-github-as-ssot.md)).

## Governing Bodies

| Body | Authority |
| --- | --- |
| **Founder Board** | Highest accountability for vision, portfolio, major decisions, and GAIOS adoption |
| **Product Office** | Stewardship of documentation integrity, registers, templates, and standards currency |
| **AI Governance (steward role)** | Rules for AI collaboration, session quality, and hallucination controls |

Founders: Gomathi K (Founder & CEO), Gowtham (Co-Founder), Arul Jeni (Co-Founder).

## Principles

1. **SSOT over chat** — Durable truth lives in the repository.
2. **Additive evolution** — Do not overwrite existing approved standards without an Approved change.
3. **Human approval** — AI drafts; humans approve Status, Decisions, and external claims.
4. **Product clarity** — Subscription OS and Pawn Management remain separately scoped ([DEC-GPO-003](../decision-register/dec-gpo-003-product-portfolio-structure.md)).
5. **Privacy** — No real customer PII in GitHub docs.
6. **Traceability** — Decisions and risks use stable IDs and review dates.
7. **Navigability** — Every GAIOS document carries metadata and Parent/Child/Related/Previous/Next/Back to START-HERE links.

## Decision Rights Summary

See [Authority Matrix](./authority-matrix.md) for detailed RACI. Material company decisions follow [Approval Workflow](./approval-workflow.md).

## AI Collaboration Rules (summary)

- AI assistants are collaborators, not authorities.
- Material outputs require verification against SSOT or named sources.
- Agents must respect file-mutation restrictions in their briefs.
- See Risk [RISK-GPO-002](../risk-register/risk-gpo-002-ai-hallucination-as-source.md).

## Amendment

Amendments to this charter require Founder Board Approval and a new version bump with Last Updated date.
