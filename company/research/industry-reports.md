## Source Handling Rules

1. Store **citations and summaries** here; store licensed PDFs in an approved private archive if purchased.
2. Record **publisher, year, and access date** for every cited claim.
3. Prefer primary regulators and reputable analyst/industry associations over unverified blogs.
4. Mark confidence when a claim is secondary (analyst) vs primary (regulator, earnings, public filing).

### Jurisdiction note

Pawn and collateral lending rules vary sharply by region. Industry reports are **inputs** to a compliance matrix; they are not legal advice. Product claims about compliance require counsel review before market publication.
