## Privacy and Ethics Rules

1. **No real customer PII** in this repository (names, emails, phones, store addresses, government IDs, account numbers).
2. Use **segment labels** only (e.g., `SOS-INT-003 · Mid-market SaaS RevOps`).
3. Store raw recordings and contact lists outside GitHub in an approved private store; link only by interview ID.
4. Obtain consent for notes use; do not publish quotes that identify a person or company without written approval.

## Interview Framework

| Phase | Duration | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Context | 5 min | Role, company stage, tools in use |
| Jobs & workflows | 20 min | Day-in-the-life of billing or pawn ops |
| Pain & workarounds | 15 min | Failures, cost of errors, shadow systems |
| Buying criteria | 10 min | Who signs, what blocks purchase |
| Wrap | 5 min | Permission to follow up; referral ask |

### Script themes — Subscription OS

- How plans, trials, upgrades, and cancellations are handled today
- Who owns invoice disputes and failed payments
- Where usage data lives and how it becomes a charge
- What “good enough” reporting means for finance vs product

### Script themes — Pawn Management

- Ticket creation speed and required fields on the counter
- Collateral intake, appraisal, storage, and chain of custody
- Renewal, redemption, and forfeiture workflows
- Reporting for compliance and owner visibility across stores
