## Canonical business glossary

Shared terms (ARR, MRR, CAC, LTV, ICP, etc.) live in [../../glossary/business-terms.md](../../glossary/business-terms.md). Use those definitions unless a Decision supersedes them.

## Subscription OS business terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Active subscription** | Billable recurring relationship in a non-canceled state |
| **ARPU** | Average Revenue Per User/account over a period |
| **Net revenue retention (NRR)** | Expansion minus contraction/churn on existing cohort |
| **Failed payment rate** | Share of recurring charges that do not succeed on first attempt |
| **Recovery rate** | Share of failed payments later collected via dunning |
| **Plan catalog** | Sellable commercial packages and add-ons |
| **Usage-based pricing** | Charges driven by metered consumption |
| **Hybrid pricing** | Combination of seats/base fee and usage |
| **Revenue under management** | Customer revenue processed/tracked by the billing system |
| **RevOps** | Revenue operations function bridging sales, finance, and product |

## Pawn Management business terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Principal** | Amount lent against collateral |
| **Finance charge / interest** | Cost of the loan per applicable rules and disclosures |
| **Loan-to-value (LTV)** | Loan amount relative to appraised collateral value (context differs from SaaS LTV) |
| **Ticket volume** | Count of active or periodic loan tickets |
| **Redeem rate** | Share of tickets redeemed vs forfeited |
| **Inventory turn (forfeits)** | Speed of selling forfeited goods |
| **Store contribution** | Location-level profit contribution after direct costs |
| **Owner-operator** | Buyer persona running one or few stores hands-on |
| **Multi-store chain** | Operator with centralized oversight across locations |
| **Compliance pack** | Commercial module for jurisdiction reporting features |

## Disambiguation notes

| Ambiguous label | SaaS meaning | Pawn meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **LTV** | Customer lifetime value | Loan-to-value on collateral |
| **Ticket** | Support ticket (avoid in SOS billing docs) | Loan ticket (primary PAW meaning) |
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue | Also a document prefix for Market Research Report — disambiguate in prose |
