## What is GAIOS?

GAIOS (Gojen AI Operating System) is the internal operating system for how Gojen Technology works—documentation, decisions, risks, research, founder rituals, and AI collaboration. It is not Subscription OS or Pawn Management software.

## Where do I start?

Open [START-HERE.md](../START-HERE.md), then the company INDEX/SEARCH guides when available.

## Is ChatGPT or Cursor the source of truth?

No. They are collaborators. The GitHub repository is SSOT ([DEC-GPO-001](../decision-register/dec-gpo-001-github-as-ssot.md)).

## Can AI approve a decision?

No. Humans approve. AI may draft options using [decision-draft-template.md](../templates/decision-draft-template.md).

## Why two products?

Founder Board adopted a dual-active portfolio: Subscription OS and Pawn Management ([DEC-GPO-003](../decision-register/dec-gpo-003-product-portfolio-structure.md)). Mitigate focus risk via weekly primary-product rule ([RISK-GPO-003](../risk-register/risk-gpo-003-dual-product-focus.md)).

## Where does market research live?

Company-level synthesis: [Research Center](../research/README.md). Product deep-dives may later use `products/*/02-market-research/`.

## May I put customer names in the repo?

No. Use anonymized interview IDs and segments ([Customer Interviews](../research/customer-interviews.md)).

## How do I change an Approved document?

Follow [Approval Workflow](../governance/approval-workflow.md). Do not silently overwrite meaning; bump version and update Last Updated.

## What is the difference between `company/templates/` and root `templates/`?

- Root `templates/`: Product Office lifecycle templates (PRD, BRD, etc.).
- `company/templates/`: GAIOS ritual templates (AI sessions, founder logs, agent briefs).
