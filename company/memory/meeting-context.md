## AI meeting rules (summary)

1. AI-generated notes are drafts until a human meeting owner accepts them.
2. Do not invent attendance, votes, or approvals.
3. Capture material decisions through [DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md](../ai-governance/DECISION-LIFECYCLE.md).
4. Prefer linking pre-reads: START-HERE, CURRENT-SPRINT, product READMEs.
5. After meetings, update memory or board pack only when facts actually changed.

## Session tip

If an AI session is asked to “catch up on meetings,” start from the minutes folders listed above, then summarize with citations to file paths rather than relying on chat recollection.
