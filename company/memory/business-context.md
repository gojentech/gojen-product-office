## Business posture

Gojen Technology operates a Product Office that turns founder intent into governed product work. Value is created by shipping clear product doctrine and executable product systems — starting with Subscription OS — while keeping decisions recoverable in GitHub.

Commercial detail (pricing, ICP, business model canvases, BRDs) belongs under product business folders, especially:

- [products/subscription-os/03-business/](../../products/subscription-os/03-business/README.md)
- Related discovery and market research stages under Subscription OS

This memory file does not invent market numbers or pricing. It records how business work is organized.

---

## Priority logic

1. Founder Board sets company and portfolio priority.
2. Subscription OS is the primary product investment.
3. Pawn Management remains an active workspace without displacing Subscription OS by default.
4. GAIOS investment (Sprint GAIOS-1) improves operating leverage for all products by reducing AI/human context loss.
