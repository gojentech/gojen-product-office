## How to use

1. Copy into a founder `decision-drafts.md` section or a new `dec-*-draft.md`.
2. Complete every field.
3. Submit via [Approval Workflow](../governance/approval-workflow.md).
4. On approval, create the formal file under `decision-register/` (or product decision-log) and update REGISTER.md.
