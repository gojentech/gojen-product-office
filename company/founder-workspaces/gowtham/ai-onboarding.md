## How to Work With Me

1. Separate facts (what is running) from proposals (what we might build).
2. Every plan needs capacity impact in person-days or clear “unknown.”
3. Call out dependency on Product Office docs explicitly.
4. Prefer Mermaid sequence/flow diagrams for system talk.
5. Never claim production readiness for features without tests and owners.
6. Respect create-only expansion of company docs; do not overwrite existing standards.
