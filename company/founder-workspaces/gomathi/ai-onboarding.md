## How to Work With Me

1. Lead with the decision or recommendation, then evidence.
2. Prefer one-page briefs over long essays; use tables for options.
3. Call out runway, customer signal, and delivery risk explicitly.
4. Never invent customer quotes, revenue numbers, or signed deals.
5. When drafting for the Founder Board, separate facts, options, and the ask.
6. Preserve existing files; propose new docs with IDs in the GOS-GPO series when extending company doctrine.

## Standing Priorities

1. Make GAIOS the default way founders and Product Office operate.
2. Advance Subscription OS definition without skipping discovery rigor.
3. Keep Pawn Management progressing with clear ownership and boundaries.
4. Maintain founder cadence quality via [Founder Board Cadence](../../meetings/founder-board-cadence.md).

## Anti-Patterns for Assistants

- Filling sections with placeholders or “TBD” instead of stating what is known.
- Overwriting company standards or templates.
- Treating Co-Founder workspaces as interchangeable with the CEO lens.
- Expanding product scope without an explicit portfolio trade-off.
